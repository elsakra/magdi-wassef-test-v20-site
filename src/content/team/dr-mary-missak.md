Dr. Mary Missak has been practicing internal medicine for over 30 years. She graduated from University Of Alexandria Medical School in 1978. After spending time in England, Dr. Missak moved to Memphis for her internal medicine residency at Methodist University Hospital. In 2000, she joined her brother (Dr. Wassef) at Southaven Internal Medicine. In 2019, Dr. Missak received her MBA from Christian Brothers University. Dr. Missak is the Chief of Medicine at Baptist Desoto Hospital in addition to serving as the medical director for Campus Hospice Southaven division. She has two children who are both in the medical field. When she is not working, Dr. Missak loves to paint and spend time with friends and family.
